About Bistro Raffo

Bistro Raffo is a 40-seat modern bistro blending Italian flavours with fresh British ingredients. We focus on seasonal menus, honest cooking, and a relaxed atmosphere that welcomes everyone, including wheelchair users and families.

Our concept was developed as a complete catering operations plan, covering everything from food safety and staffing to sustainability, technology, and financial planning.

Core values: Fresh • Seasonal • Accessible • Sustainable • Professional

Sustainability & Food Waste Reduction

Bistro Raffo operates with a strong sustainability plan:

  • Local suppliers to reduce food miles.
  • Vegetable trims used for stocks and soups.
  • Stale bread turned into croutons and breadcrumbs.
  • Used oil collected by specialist recycling contractors.
  • Fruit and vegetable waste sent for composting.
  • Safe, surplus food offered to local charities where possible.

Takeaway packaging is recyclable or compostable, and we monitor energy and water usage each month.

Holidays, Sick Days & Lateness

  • Holiday accrues according to UK law (minimum 5.6 weeks pro rata).
  • Sick days must be reported as early as possible before shift; self-certification up to 7 days.
  • Repeated no-shows or lateness may lead to a formal warning, then final warning, and possible dismissal.

Applying for a Job

  1. Send CV and short cover message to jobs@bistroraffo.co.uk.
  2. Attend trial shift and interview.
  3. Provide proof of right to work and references.

Leaving Your Job

Staff are asked to provide at least 2 weeks' written notice (4 weeks for senior roles). Exit interviews may be offered to collect feedback.

Key Policies

Customer Damage Policy

If a customer intentionally or negligently damages property, we may charge a replacement fee:

  • Plate: £3–£5
  • Glass: £2–£4
  • Chair: £30–£60
  • Light fitting: £50–£120

All incidents are documented in the incident log.

Accessibility

  • Wheelchair ramp at entrance.
  • Accessible toilet with rails and alarm cord.
  • Space left between tables for wheelchair turning.
  • Allergy information available on request.

Safety, HACCP & Checklists

Fire Safety

  • CO2 and wet-chemical extinguishers in the kitchen.
  • Fire blanket near the cooking line.
  • Clearly marked fire exits and assembly point.

Opening Checklist (Summary)

  • Check fridge and freezer temperatures.
  • Inspect for signs of pests.
  • Ensure hand-wash stations have soap and towels.
  • Check extraction system and gas supply.

Closing Checklist (Summary)

  • Deep clean key areas.
  • Empty bins and secure waste outside.
  • Turn off gas and non-essential electrics.
  • Lock chemical cupboard and back doors.

Pest & Fault Procedure

Any sign of pests (droppings, gnaw marks) leads to immediate isolation of the area, deep cleaning, and contacting pest control. Equipment faults are logged, labelled as out of order, and reported to maintenance.
